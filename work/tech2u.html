Background

In the summer of 2022, I spent 12 weeks with the Learning Space Management and Innovation Hub team, where I engineered Tech2U, a support portal for instructors to request technical help in classrooms.

Opportunity

As classrooms are re-designed with more innovative learning tools, instructors, with their busy schedule, can no longer keep up with the varied technology of different spaces. Therefore, there is a trained team of students and experienced technicians available to support them. Bridging communication gap and spreading the words the program, we welcome Tech2U to life!
