ReCov-2gether

UX Designer/ Software Engineer/ Student Engagement Award/ 2021

Shengye (Tony) Niu, Anna Lai, Kimlin Chin, Lucia Guintu & Winsy Leung

Overview

ReCoV-2gether is an app developed for the communities at the University of Toronto with an aim to raise awareness about the latest Covid-19 information in the Toronto neighbourhood, provide mental health resources and cultivate a platform where meaningful stories are shared and encouraged. Hosting eleven podcasts with guests coming from different backgrounds, gathering articles written by members of the university sharing their two cents on a passion topic, comparing different data sets to draw in important Covid-19 information, the app strives to be a safe space for reliable information, helpful resources and stories sharing in the time of many changes.
